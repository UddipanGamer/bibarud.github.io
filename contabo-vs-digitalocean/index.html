What is Contabo?

Contabo is a German hosting company which was founded in 2003. It has grown to be one of the three largest web hosting providers in Germany. Contabo is known for their competitive prices and focus on standard, business-grade hosting features.

In the beginning, Contabo was just a small start-up, but it has since grown into a player that needs to be taken seriously. In fact, they have many data centers around the world with over 1 million customers from many different countries and industries.

What’s special about Contabo?

There are a couple of things that make Contabo stand out from the competition. The first is the price. It’s not always easy to find a reliable, affordable hosting provider, but with Contabo you can rest assured that they will not overcharge you. In fact, they provide the most competitive prices in comparison to other providers. What’s more, their customer service is excellent and guarantees high-quality support 24/7/365 days a year. As for their server performance and uptime, it’s second to none with 99% up-time on all servers.

They also offer SSL certificates which come at no extra cost and have a 99% uptime on all servers as well as excellent customer service 24/7/365.

What is Digital Ocean?

Digital Ocean is a cloud hosting service that offers a hosting platform with a simple user interface.

Digital Ocean provides a platform for developers to create and deploy code in the form of a web server, database server, and other applications. The cloud server is scalable and provides a number of features such as data backups and monitoring.

Digital Ocean is a cloud-hosted virtual computer platform used for running and managing apps, websites, and other services. It offers a computing environment for developers to create and deploy code in the form of a web server, database server, and other applications. The cloud server is scalable and provides a number of features such as load balancing, object storage, firewalls, snapshots, and more.

What’s special about Digital Ocean?

DigitalOcean is a cloud hosting service provider that specializes in infrastructure for application developers.

DigitalOcean offers the latest Linux operating systems and software packages through its control panel, one-click application installs, and its interface.

The company has over 10 million registered customers globally with over 1 million customers using DigitalOcean as their primary web hosting provider.

Whats The Difference Between Contabo and DigitalOcean?

Contabo and DigitalOcean are two of the most popular cloud hosting service providers in the world. However, there is a difference between them that separates them from each other. Let’s take a closer look at what this difference is and when it might become useful to you.

When we talk about cloud hosting, we’re typically referring to hosting your website in a data center in order to provide you with faster and more reliable access to your site, especially in the case of an outage, such as when your internet connection goes out.

DigitalOcean and Contabo both offer this type of hosting service among many other similar services for companies who want their websites hosted on the internet.

Comparing Performance & Speed

Both the companies have a lot of similarities and differences. For example, both the companies provide virtual private servers, but Contabo is a German company while DigitalOcean is a US firm.

The basic difference between the two is that Contabo offers only few configuration of VPS with many features while DigitalOcean offers many different types of VPS with varied features. In our speed test we found Digital Ocean and contabo to be relatable to each other.

Contabo has more ram than Digital Ocean for $2 more, but contabo doesn’t have pure SSD Speeds. Their Servers are configured as boosted HDD meaning, SSD + HDD. In contabo both HDD and SDD are used as a hybrid manner, the OS and the other importnat things are stored on SSD but the other less important things are stored on their HDD.

Whereas in Digital Ocean the default is SSD, which means better read and write speeds. But as contabo has more ram they are both similar in terms of performance and speeds.

Conclusion : Which One to Choose for Hosting Your Business Website?

Digital ocean, a cloud hosting service provider, and Contabo, a German hosting service provider, are comparable in many ways. But they also have some distinct advantages and drawbacks.

For starters, both offer a wide range of services to suit the needs of small businesses. They have competitive pricing for VPS hosting and have generous free trial periods that allow you to test their services before committing to them. Contabo offers customer support channels including phone, chat and email. While digital ocean has a ticket system.

The biggest difference between these two providers is that Digital Ocean has a more advanced infrastructure than Contabo which means that its servers are more reliable and faster.

For people looking for a more reliable hosting provider that can be scaled easily in the future Digital Ocean would be the right choice. But it doesn’t mean that Contabo is bad.

For people on a very tight budget and want to start their website and needs more RAM and CPU power for a low price, Contabo is the perfect choice.
