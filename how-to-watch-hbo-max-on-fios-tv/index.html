What exactly is FiOS?

According to Google, FiOS is an abbreviation for Fiber Optics Service, provided by Verizon as a "bundled Internet access, telephone, and television service that operates over a fiber-optic communications network with over 5 million customers in nine U.S. states" over a fiber-optic communications network.

So, what exactly is the problem with HBO Max on FiOS TV?

You would expect this to function the same way it does on Roku, Apple TV, and other streaming services. Verizon, on the other hand, is not like that. The main reason for this is that the HBO Max app is not included in the setup box that they supply for their media provider, FiOS, which is the most popular option.

It does not support the HBO Max app, so there is no way to get one on this device. Some readers may find this extremely unusual, given that Verizon, in particular, has said that HBO Max is supported. However, there is a catch to this.

HBO Max is supported on Verizon's network but not on the company's FiOS network. This means that you can acquire an HBO Max subscription through Verizon, and it will work perfectly well, but it will not work on FiOS TV because they do not have the HBO Max app available on their platform at this time.
